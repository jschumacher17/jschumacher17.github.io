What to do

Atlanta has plenty to do to keep you busy while you are here

Here are some of my favorite things to do in the city...

The World of Coke

Enjoy one of Atlanta's most famous attractions and home to the secrete Coca-Cola formula. Please note that as a Coca-Cola employee, I am able to get discounted tickets to The World of Coke

World of Coke

Georgia Aquarium

Enjoy the largest aquarium in the Western Hemisphere. It houses more than 120,000 animals, representing 500 species in 8.5 million gallons of water. There are 60 different habitats with 12,000 sqft of viewing windows.

Georgia Aquarium

National Center for Civil and Human Rights

The Center for Civil and Human Rights connects the American Civil Rights Movement to today's Global Human Rights Movements.

Human Rights Center
